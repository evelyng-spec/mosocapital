We train the next generation of bold investors

Moso Capital is the University of Pennsylvania's premier experiential venture capital program. By mirroring an investment fund, we empower top undergraduates to gain hands-on investing experience while supporting our venture and angel investors in sourcing and conducting diligence on the most exciting startups.

We build thoughtful connections between bold founders and sharp investors, acting as a trusted first touchpoint and long-term partner in the venture process.

At Moso, relationships come first.

A partnership with Moso isn’t just a short-term project — it’s a lasting commitment to the founders we believe in and the investors we work alongside to bring their visions to life.

Frequently asked questions

Who is on the deal team?

Each deal team works exclusively with an investor (i.e., an investor from a corporate venture capital firm or angel) and is led by 1 team leader (Managing Director) in addition to 4 other analysts.

Who are the investors Moso partners with?

Moso works with seed to Series A investors across different industries—from biotech to B2B SaaS and AI agents to consumer tech. A few of Moso’s partnering investors included investors from Menlo Ventures, General Catalyst, and Insight Partners.

What do you do when working with an investor?

The scope of work mainly depends on the investor you work with, though you will all be sourcing (looking for startups aligning with investor’s thesis) and writing up thesis primers. Additionally, alongside their deal team, analysts will have the chance to pitch the best company the team has sourced during our GBM.

How often do teams meet?

Teams have internal weekly meetings and bi-weekly meetings with their respective partnering investors. Moso also hosts bi-weekly GBMs where upperclassmen will go through anything from investing case studies / frameworks, deals to recruiting advice.

How is the Moso community?

From cafe hopping to board games night to happy hours, Moso prides itself on cultivating a flat culture where upperclassmen and underclassmen are more than just teammates. Join us to experience it yourself!
