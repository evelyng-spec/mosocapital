Teammates, friends, and community of doers.

We build, invest, and learn together — but we also laugh a lot along the way. From career treks to board game nights to morning runs, Moso is where ambitious students find teammates, friends, and a second family.

Education curriculum

At Moso, we welcome beginners and teach you everything from the ground up in our academy bootcamp: the different stages of venture capital, how to source deals, how to conduct due diligence, and how to write and present a full investment memo. Our curriculum is rigorous, hands-on, and designed to help you think like an investor — culminating in pitching a real startup to Moso board. If you show up hungry to learn, we’ll meet you there.

Collaborative Approach

Every semester, Moso takes students to a career trek. While we’re rooted in venture, our career treks open doors across industries — from investment banking and growth equity to early-stage startups and top VC firms. We spend a day in New York visiting 3-4 offices, meeting professionals, and learning what these careers actually look like. It’s a chance to explore paths you might not have considered, build your network, and bond with your Moso teammates over new neighborhoods and late-night bites. No application, no cut — if you’re part of Moso, you’re welcome to come.

The Moso community

A lot of us joined Moso as freshmen. What we found was more than just a club. We found people who’d become our closest friends: from classmates to bigs and littles. From intense cards against humanity rounds to supporting each other through recruiting or late-night Kiwi runs, Moso knows how to have fun and shows up for one another.
