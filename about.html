Our Model

Moso Capital was founded with one core belief: the best way to learn venture is to do venture. We operate as a hands-on investing program that mirrors the structure and rigor of a traditional fund. Our students lead sourcing, diligence, and founder calls—bringing fresh energy, sharp analysis, and curiosity to every conversation.

But we don’t act alone. Our venture and angel investor partners play an integral role in the process—offering strategic feedback, helping prioritize leads, and supporting decisions with real capital. This feedback loop ensures Moso adds meaningful value at every step, while giving students access to insights few others their age get to experience.

Our Team

At Moso, we operate as one team—with shared conviction, trust, and deep collaboration. Each deal is run by a small, agile group of student investors with support from experienced partners. We welcome diverse thinking and encourage open debate, believing that the best ideas emerge from discussion and shared learning.

Together, we’re building a new kind of investing model—one powered by curiosity, discipline, and execution.
