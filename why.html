Why Moso?

At Moso, we believe strong partnerships are built on trust, conviction, and follow-through. We’re not just here to support — we’re here to invest deeply in the work, building real relationships with founders and investors that last far beyond a single deal cycle.

Dynamic Sourcing

We’re proactive, bold, and unapologetically persistent. If we’re excited about a founder or company, we’ll go out of our way to connect. We move fast, adapt quickly, and lean on both the Moso and Penn networks to creatively source the most compelling opportunities.

Collaborative Approach

We don’t overpromise — we execute. From the start of the semester, each team sets aligned expectations and works directly with our investor partner. In our first semester, we sourced and closed a deal. That’s the standard we hold ourselves to.

1:1 Relationship

Every team is assigned to one investor — and that’s who they work with from day one. We’re selective because we care about building true venture partnerships grounded in mutual trust, aligned theses, and long-term commitment.

Collegiate

As a Gen-Z-powered organization, we see trends early and think differently. We collaborate with students at Harvard and other top universities, receive inbound deal flow from across the country, and bring fresh ideas backed by real energy. It’s not just about being young — it’s about being plugged in.
